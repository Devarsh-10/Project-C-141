AI PING PONG GAME







Source Code : Devarsh || Game Credit : Ping Pong

Instructions

1. Keep the screen of your laptop straight

2. Keep your wrist atleast 3-4 feet away from the camera

3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and descending order, a red dot should appear on your wrist

4. Press the play button, keeping your wrist 3-4 feet away

5. The right paddle on the left is our paddle, that will move as per your movement of wrist
